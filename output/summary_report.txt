
RAILWAY DATA ANALYSIS REPORT
----------------------------
KEY INSIGHTS:

1. Major source and destination stations handle the bulk of traffic.
2. Weekdays have higher train operations compared to weekends.
3. Heatmap shows which routes are heavily used.
